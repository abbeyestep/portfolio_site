Dwight Schrute

Print

The Office is my favorite show, and it would be nothing without Dwight Schrute. I made this diorama in his honor. Each layer was drawn in Adobe Illustrator and then printed and assembled. The quote on a back layer reads:

"I don't have a lot of experience with vampires, but I have hunted werewolves. I shot one once, but by the time I got to it, it had turned back into my neighbor's dog."
